1. Overview

DropCalcPro ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website dropcalcpro.in (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service, and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Unless otherwise defined in this Privacy Policy, terms used have the same meanings as in our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us through our contact form or email, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Subject and message content
  • Any other information you choose to include

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you access and use the Service, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution
  • Log Data: IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, referral URLs, and timestamps
  • Usage Data: Calculator inputs, currency selections, and feature interactions (stored locally in your browser)
  • Location Data: General geographic location derived from your IP address (country/region level only)

2.3 Local Storage

All calculator data, preferences, and inputs are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service — Ensuring the calculator functions correctly across devices and browsers
  • To improve user experience — Analyzing usage patterns to optimize performance and add relevant features
  • To respond to enquiries — Replying to support requests, feature suggestions, and business partnerships
  • To detect and prevent abuse — Identifying and blocking malicious traffic or automated abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations — Fulfilling regulatory requirements when applicable

4. Cookies and Tracking

DropCalcPro uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site. These are anonymized and do not identify individuals.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your currency selection and display preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the Service.

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. Currently, we use:

6. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmission occurs over HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher)
  • Server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days
  • Access to server infrastructure is restricted and logged
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy:

  • Server logs: Automatically purged after 90 days

  • Local browser data: Controlled entirely by you through browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dropcalcpro@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from Children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.
