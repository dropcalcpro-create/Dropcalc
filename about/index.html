Our Story

From a Google Sheet to a Command Center

In 2023, we were running COD dropshipping campaigns across South Asia with nothing but a shared spreadsheet and a prayer. Every morning meant manually calculating ROAS, delivery rates, and net profit — and every evening meant discovering we had scaled a losing campaign by 40%.

The tools that existed were either too generic (basic profit calculators) or too enterprise (bloated BI dashboards that cost more than our ad spend). We needed something in the middle: precise, fast, and built specifically for the unique economics of COD dropshipping.

So we built it. What started as an internal tool quickly spread across our network of media buyers. Within six months, over 2,000 operators were using it daily. That is when we knew we had to make it available to everyone.
